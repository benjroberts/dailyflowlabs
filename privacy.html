Legal & Transparency

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026 | Entity: Daily Flow Labs LLC

1. Introduction

Daily Flow Labs LLC ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is dedicated to protecting personal information collected through our software applications, suite of utilities (including DomusDash, IronDial, ThumbVerify, LocalRedactPDF, ShortCodeIcons, and associated tools), and our website located at dailyflowlabs.com.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and safeguard it, and your rights regarding your personal data when interacting with our software products and paid services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to deliver high-performance, privacy-first software products:

  • Account & Billing Information: When you purchase subscriptions or digital products, we collect your email address, customer name, and transaction identifiers. Payment card details are processed directly and securely by our payment processor, Stripe. Daily Flow Labs LLC does not store or process full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Local-First & On-Device Processing: Many of our applications operate on a local-first architecture (e.g., local PDF redact tools, biometric algorithms, or offline family tools). Data created within local utilities remains stored directly in your browser or device memory.
  • Analytics & Telemetry: We collect aggregated, non-identifiable usage analytics via tools such as Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to identify software bugs, analyze page responsiveness, and enhance platform performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We utilize collected information strictly for legitimate operational purposes:

  • To process payment transactions, manage subscription tiers, and issue receipts via Stripe.
  • To provide customer support, respond to user inquiries, and deliver software updates.
  • To send administrative notifications regarding terms updates or service alerts.
  • To maintain site security, prevent fraudulent activity, and comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Third-Party Data Processors

We partner with trusted third-party service providers to facilitate infrastructure and payment capabilities. These providers access data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf:

  • Stripe: Payment processing and subscription management. Privacy policy available at stripe.com/privacy.
  • Google Analytics & Microsoft Clarity: Aggregated traffic monitoring and user experience diagnostics.

5. Data Security & Retention

We enforce modern industry security protocols to safeguard your information. All communications with our web services employ TLS/SSL encryption in transit. We retain account and billing records for as long as necessary to fulfill contractual obligations, resolve disputes, and comply with tax and financial recordkeeping laws.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You maintain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access & Deletion: The right to request access to or deletion of your personal data stored in our system systems.
  • Communication Opt-Out: Unsubscribing from marketing news or updates at any time via the link in our emails.
